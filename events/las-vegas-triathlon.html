LAS VEGAS TRIATHLON

October 10, 2026 · Boulder City, NV · Free for spectators

A multisport competition utilizing Boulder Beach at Lake Mead as a swim-to-bike transition hub. Spectators gather at the finish-line race village to watch participants navigate the final miles along the paved River Mountain Loop trail.

Best for: Endurance sports fans and families supporting participants.

PLANNING NOTES
  • Wave starts begin at 6:30 AM — arrive by 6:00 AM to secure a viewing spot on the rocky shoreline.
  • Sturdy, closed-toe footwear is required for navigating the gravel and rock surfaces of Boulder Beach.
  • There is no natural shade at the race village; bring portable umbrellas and high-SPF sunscreen.
ACCESSIBILITY

The race village sits on paved lots and hard-packed dirt; accessible parking provides paved access to the transition area.

Where to Stay Near Boulder City

Closest to the venue: Boulder Dam Hotel, Boulder City Inn, and Best Western Hoover Dam Hotel in Boulder City. For casino-resort stays with member-rate booking, the Henderson resorts below are roughly 25 minutes away.

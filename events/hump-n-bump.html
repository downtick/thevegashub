An off-road gathering at the Clark County Fairgrounds where 4×4 vehicles navigate rock obstacles in the Logandale Trails. Spectators use sandstone ridges as natural platforms to watch drivers tackle technical ledges and deep sand washes.

Best for: Off-road enthusiasts and fans of technical rock crawling.

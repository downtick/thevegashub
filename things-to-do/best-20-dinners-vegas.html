BEST $20 DINNERS IN LAS VEGAS

15 best dinners in Las Vegas for under $20 — off-Strip Chinese, downtown burgers, Italian hole-in-the-wall, and the cheap-eats spots locals actually eat at.

01

Battista's Hole in the Wall

$45 dinner with bottomless wine — not under $20 per person if you drink, but worth listing. Sandwich at lunch is $14.

02

In-N-Out Burger

Every Vegas tourist denies wanting it. Every local has been at 1am. Under $10.

03

Tacos El Gordo (multiple)

$3 tacos al pastor. Dinner for $15, fully stuffed.

04

Pho Kim Long (Chinatown)

Huge bowl of pho under $15.

05

Monta Ramen (Chinatown)

Best tonkotsu for $18.

06

Pepperoni's Pizza at Planet Hollywood

Giant slice for under $8.

07

Secret Pizza at Cosmopolitan

Same deal. Under $10 a slice, open late.

08

Gold Fork Burger Bar

Downtown. Gourmet burger under $15.

09

Raising Cane's

24/7 chicken fingers combo under $12. Open late.

10

Nora's Italian Cuisine

West-side old-school Italian. Pasta dinners under $20.

11

District One Kitchen

Chinatown Vietnamese. Combo plates under $18.

12

Earl of Sandwich (Planet Hollywood)

Cabo sandwich + chips + drink under $14.

13

Shake Shack

Don't be snobby. $15 combo is fine.

14

Du-par's

Downtown. Breakfast-for-dinner under $18. Pancakes and bacon.

15

Nacho Daddy

Downtown. Massive nachos to share under $20 per person.

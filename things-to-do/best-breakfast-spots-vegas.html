BEST BREAKFAST SPOTS IN LAS VEGAS

The best breakfast spots in Las Vegas — hangover fixes, brunch standouts, Strip breakfasts worth their price, and the locals' morning picks.

01

Hash House A Go Go (Plaza + Linq)

Biggest portions in the city. The sage fried chicken benedict is the move.

02

Eggslut at Cosmopolitan

Sandwich-driven. The Fairfax is famous for good reason.

03

The Henry at The Cosmopolitan

24-hour menu. Chicken & waffles, famous burger. $30 average.

04

The Kitchen at The Wynn

High-end à la carte. Best eggs benedict on the Strip. $45.

05

Bouchon at The Venetian

Thomas Keller's bistro. Weekend brunch is the Vegas expense-account brunch.

06

Black Tap at The Venetian

Shake + burger for breakfast. $20 freak-out shakes.

07

Skinny Fats Happy

Off-Strip. Two menus — healthy vs happy. Always-packed locals' brunch.

08

Mr Mamas Breakfast & Lunch

Off-Strip chain. Chicken-fried everything. Loved by locals.

09

The Peppermill coffeeshop

Icons. Fruit plate, 24-hour menu, next to Resorts World.

10

Bacchanal Brunch at Caesars

Adds breakfast to the buffet rotation on Sat/Sun. Worth the price.

11

Oscar's at the Plaza (downtown)

Old-school downtown breakfast with a Strip-view from the dome.

12

Du-par's at Golden Gate

Pancakes since 1938. Thick-cut bacon. Downtown.

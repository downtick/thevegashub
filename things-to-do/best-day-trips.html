THINGS TO DO

BEST DAY TRIPS FROM LAS VEGAS

The best day trips from Las Vegas — Grand Canyon, Hoover Dam, Red Rock, Valley of Fire, Death Valley, and Zion. Drive times + booking links.

01

Grand Canyon West Rim (Skywalk)

2h15m each way · The closest Grand Canyon view from Vegas. Skywalk is the attention-grabber; Guano Point is the actual view.

02

Grand Canyon South Rim

4h15m each way · Longer drive, bigger canyon. Worth the overnight if you can.

03

Hoover Dam

45 min each way · The actual dam tour is 90 minutes. Combine with Lake Mead.

04

Red Rock Canyon Scenic Loop

30 min each way · 13-mile loop, pullouts, short hikes. Our go-to Vegas morning.

05

Valley of Fire State Park

1h each way · Underrated red-rock state park. Less crowded than Red Rock, more dramatic.

06

Mount Charleston

45 min each way · Pine trees, snow in winter, 30 degrees cooler in summer. Vegas's local reset.

07

Death Valley National Park

2h each way · October-April only. Lowest point in North America, wildest terrain.

08

Zion National Park

2h45m each way · Best done as an overnight in Springdale, UT. Drives you want to do.

09

Seven Magic Mountains

25 min each way · Giant neon-rock art installation. 15-minute stop, great photo.

10

Laughlin on the Colorado River

1h30m each way · Cheap rooms, beach, jet skis, buffets. Great weekend away from Vegas.

11

Area 51 / Extraterrestrial Highway

2h30m each way · Weird roadside stop. You won't see aliens. You will see a lot of Joshua trees.

12

Bryce Canyon

3h45m each way · Push-yourself long day. Most photogenic national park in the Southwest.

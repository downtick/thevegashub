BEST VEGAS F1 HOTELS

Best hotels for the Las Vegas F1 Grand Prix 2026 — rooms with track views, walkable to the pit, and where to get the best member rates.

01

Wynn Las Vegas

Inside the track. North-facing rooms look directly down the main straight. Views + no traffic.

02

Encore at Wynn

Same campus as Wynn — suites with track views without the Wynn price.

03

Venetian

Track wraps around the front drive. Palazzo side is quieter.

04

Palazzo

Same footprint as Venetian. All-suite = easier for a 4-night F1 weekend.

05

The Cosmopolitan

Terrace Suites face the pit and main straight. Book a terrace and you don't need a grandstand ticket.

06

Waldorf Astoria

North-facing rooms above floor 20 = track views without the hotel-inside-track insanity.

07

Fontainebleau

New for F1. North end of the Strip, close to Turn 14.

08

Bellagio

Faces the track across the fountains. Spectacular.

09

Caesars Palace

Track runs right along the property. Balcony suites sell out first.

10

Paris Las Vegas

Eiffel Tower observation deck becomes a grandstand during F1 weekend.

11

Flamingo

Closer to the track than Paris. Budget F1 option if you're not here for luxury.

12

Planet Hollywood

Mid-Strip, affordable F1 stay if you don't need a track-view room.

ATOMIC GOLF
LAS VEGAS

A state-of-the-art driving range, lessons for every skill level, and multiple restaurants and bars — steps from the Strip. If you only pick one "something different" in Vegas, this is it.

RESERVE A BAY
THE RANGE

STATE-OF-THE-ART DRIVING RANGE

Enjoy a modern golfing experience with top-notch facilities and advanced technology to improve your skills — or just play some of the fun games available. This is a great place for groups: wide climate-controlled bays, tech-enabled tee boxes that track every shot, and a scoring system built to make even non-golfers compete.

LEARN THE GAME

GOLF LESSONS AVAILABLE

Improve your skills with golf lessons for all levels, from total beginners picking up a club for the first time to seasoned players looking to sharpen their swing. Lessons are paired with the same shot-tracking tech on the range, so every correction is grounded in real data — not guesswork.

NIGHT OUT

EAT, DRINK, AND PARTY

With multiple dining options and culinary spots on-site, there should be something available for everyone. Full bars, stadium-style energy on weekends, DJ nights, and a food lineup that's a serious step above typical driving-range fare. It's a great group stop — pair it with dinner on the Strip or make it the whole evening.

BOOK YOUR BAY

Bays sell out weekend nights and big-event weekends (F1, UFC, fights). Reserve ahead.
